Intro

Established in 2013 as a branch of Pi Sigma Epsilon , ZC Consulting is a strategy consulting organization run entirely by students of UC Berkeley. We are an educational non-profit focused on the academic and professional development of both our members and student community. Through our diverse backgrounds and analytical rigor, we offer cutting-edge research and powerful solutions to our clients.

Our mission is to collaborate with the goals and vision of individual businesses to develop creative solutions that satisfy the needs of our clients and their stakeholders.
